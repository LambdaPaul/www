Name            | 6502
---             | ---
Introduced      | 1975
Data Width      | 8-bit
Address Width   | 16-bit
Endianness      | Little
Registers       | 8-bit A, X, Y, Stack. 16-bit PC
Package         | 40-pin DIP
Instruction Count | 56

## Addressing Modes
- Implied
- Immediate
- Absolute
- Indexed Absolute
- Indexed Zero-Page
- Relative
- Accumulator
- Indirect, X
- Indirect, Y
